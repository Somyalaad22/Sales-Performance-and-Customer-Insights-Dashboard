# ✅ **Executive Summary – FNP Sales Analysis Dashboard**

This dashboard provides an end-to-end sales and customer behavior analysis for FNP (Ferns N Petals) using 1,000+ transactional records. The goal of the project was to uncover key insights related to revenue trends, top-performing products, delivery performance, and customer purchasing patterns across multiple occasions.

Using Excel’s advanced tools—**Power Query, Power Pivot, and DAX**—the raw dataset was transformed into a clean, structured data model that enabled interactive, real-time analysis.

### **Key Insights**

* **Total Revenue:** ₹35,20,984 generated from 1000 orders.
* **Average Delivery Time:** 5.53 days, indicating moderately efficient operations.
* **Average Customer Spend:** ₹3,520.98 per order.

### **Sales Performance Highlights**

* **Occasion-wise Revenue:** Highest revenue came from **Anniversary**, **Holi**, and **Raksha Bandhan**, showing strong seasonal demand.
* **Category-wise Revenue:** **Colors, Soft Toys, and Sweets** were top-performing categories.
* **Monthly Trends:** Peak sales occurred in **March** and **August**, indicating festival-driven spikes.

### **Customer & Product Insights**

* **Top 5 Best-Selling Products:** Magnum Set, Quia Gift, Harum Pack, Dolores Gift, Dessert Box.
* **Top 10 Cities by Orders:** Major order volumes came from cities like **Bhopal, Bilaspur, Dharwad, Guna**, and **Imphal**, identifying high-demand regions.

### **Operational Insights**

* Delivery time varied smoothly across hours and days, with no major bottlenecks.
* Slicers for **Order Date, Delivery Date, and Occasion** enable stakeholders to explore performance across timelines and product categories.

### **Business Impact**

This dashboard converts raw transactional data into actionable insights, enabling FNP to:

* Plan inventory for high-demand occasions
* Identify top-performing cities and product categories
* Optimize pricing and product bundles
* Improve delivery and customer satisfaction through data-driven decisions

✅ Recommendations for FNP Based on Sales Analysis
1️⃣ Focus on High-Revenue Occasions

Occasions like Anniversary, Raksha Bandhan, Holi, and Birthday generate the highest revenue.
Recommendation:

Launch targeted marketing campaigns before these festivals.

Offer combo packs or limited-edition gift sets.

Increase inventory for high-demand products during these periods.

2️⃣ Strengthen Product Categories With High Demand

Categories like Colors, Soft Toys, and Sweets show strong performance.
Recommendation:

Expand product variety in these categories.

Introduce premium versions and bundle offers.

Promote these items through banner ads and email campaigns.

3️⃣ Improve Sales in Low-Performing Categories

Some categories like Mugs, Plants, Raksha Bandhan-specific items show lower sales.
Recommendation:

Refresh designs or introduce new collections.

Offer discounts or “Buy 1 Get 1” deals.

Bundle lower-performing products with high-selling ones.

4️⃣ Target High-Order Cities With Local Campaigns

Cities like Bhopal, Bilaspur, Dharwad, Imphal, and Kavali are major order contributors.
Recommendation:

Add city-specific promotions or delivery offers.

Strengthen local delivery partners to improve service.

Run regional ad campaigns during festival months.

5️⃣ Reduce Delivery Time & Improve Logistics

Average delivery time = 5.53 days, which is relatively high for gifting items.
Recommendation:

Optimize courier routing and warehouse placement.

Offer same-day or 24–48 hour delivery in key cities.

Use predictive demand forecasting to pre-position inventory.

6️⃣ Capitalize on Monthly Seasonal Trends

Peak sales in March and August align with festivals.
Recommendation:

Increase stock for top-selling items during peak months.

Introduce festival-special product lines.

Run pre-festival discount campaigns.

7️⃣ Encourage Repeat Purchases

Average customer spend is ₹3,520, but many customers may order only once.
Recommendation:

Launch loyalty programs offering points or rewards.

Send personalized recommendations based on past purchases.

Offer subscription gifting plans for anniversaries and birthdays.

8️⃣ Highlight Top 5 Best-Selling Products

Top products like Magnum Set, Quia Gift, Harum Pack, Dolores Gift, etc. drive significant revenue.
Recommendation:

Feature these items on homepage banners.

Offer “Top Picks for You” suggestions on the website.

Promote them during high-traffic occasions.

9️⃣ Optimize Marketing for Specific Occasions

Each occasion has unique buying behavior.
Recommendation:

Build occasion-specific product bundles.

Run social media ads targeted by festival and audience interest.

Offer early-bird discounts for major events like Valentine’s Day.
